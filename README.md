# the double scoop üç®

A thoughtful news aggregator built to help combat media bias and political polarization.

Built with Svelte, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, Python, and scikit-learn  

## Features

[Demo](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IbclzgnYOXnvUA6ri-2cCkqdVgGrj1MM/view?usp=sharing)

### Compile Articles by Topic
Articles from different news sources across the political spectrum are aggregated and sorted by topic. Topics are summarized by key themes via tags. 

<img width="500" alt="Articles grouped by topic" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e87687a6-6639-47ed-8c9c-f7a4dc50fa97">

### Article Objectivity and Source Lean
Every article is tagged by its individual objectivity and the overall political leaning of its source, using data from [AllSides]([url](https://www.allsides.com/media-bias/media-bias-chart)).

<img width="500" alt="Article popup demonstrating source lean and objectivity" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9a35622d-b734-48a8-b2a0-cccb51625809">


### The Daily Scoop: Trending News Stories of the Day
News stories are automatically compiled from the topics trending that day in the Daily Scoop, providing an overview of the various topics. 

<img width="500" alt="The daily scoop" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1dfd9069-085e-4b1c-ba5d-be4d8665a014">

## Search by article or by topic
Users can do a keyword search by article or by topic by starting their search with "#." Articles are kept within the context of their topic so users can also see similar articles from other news sources.

<img width="500" alt="Search by article" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fff6d012-e770-4594-b1ee-f529a2c2f816"> <img width="500" alt="Search by topic" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/23dd5152-c512-42d7-a920-da0fae895db3">


### User Statistics
Users can view their individual media consumption statistics, including trends in news sites they frequent, article subjectivity, and article lean. 

<img width="500" alt="Statistics page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf3b84e2-0a13-4468-a4fc-a7a2a35d3ed3">
